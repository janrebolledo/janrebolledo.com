Privacy Policy for janrebolledo.com

I take your privacy seriously and want to be transparent about the information I collect and how it's used. This privacy policy explains what information I collect, how I use it, and your options for managing your information.

Information I Collect

When you visit JanRebolledo.com, I automatically collect certain information about your visit, such as the pages you view, the referral source that brought you to my website, your country, browser type, and device type. I use this information to improve my website and to understand how visitors interact with my content.

In addition, I use session cookies to store a unique identifier for your browsing session. This helps me keep track of your progress as you navigate my website and enables certain features, such as saving your preferences between visits. I do not use cookies to track your activity outside of my website.

I also use Microanalytics to collect and analyze data about user interactions on my website. This includes information such as pageviews, referral sources, and device types. Microanalytics provides me with insights that help me improve my website and provide a better user experience.

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

Under GDPR, I process your personal data only where I have a legal basis for doing so. The legal basis for processing your data is your consent, which is obtained through your use of my website.

I use the information I collect to:

  • Provide a better user experience
  • Monitor and analyze website usage
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests

I do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to protect my rights.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to be informed about how your personal data is being used
  • The right to access the personal data I hold about you
  • The right to have your personal data corrected if it is inaccurate or incomplete
  • The right to have your personal data erased ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict the processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal data
  • Rights in relation to automated decision making and profiling

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the details at the bottom of this policy.
